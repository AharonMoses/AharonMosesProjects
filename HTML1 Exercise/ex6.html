Chester Bennington

Biography

Link to wikipedia

Chester Charles Bennington (March 20, 1976 - July 20, 2017)
was an American singer and songwriter who served as the lead
vocalist of rock band Linkin Park. He was also a lead vocalist of Grey Daze,
Dead by Sunrise and Stone Temple Pilots at various points.
